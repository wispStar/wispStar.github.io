## Abstract
The present invention discloses a rat facial pain recognition method and system based on a pain relevance correction model, the method comprising: (1) acquiring a number of rat facial images and labels corresponding to identifying whether the rats are in pain or not; (2) constructing a pain relevance weight deep learning model, said model comprising a facial localized feature extraction module, a facial feature weighting and fusion module, and a pain recognition module; (3) entering each rat facial image and the corresponding label as a sample to be input into said pain associativity weight deep learning model for training; (4) inputting the facial image of the rat to be recognized into the trained pain associativity weight deep learning model, and recognizing whether the rat's face is painful or not. The present invention recognizes the rat pain or not in a non-invasive manner with a high degree of automation and high accuracy.
