# Teacher Jin’s activity record in March 2024

## College of Life Sciences Student Union May 4th Council

The May 4th Council of the Student Union of the School of Life Sciences in 2023 is mainly composed of two parts: studying the May 4th spirit and selecting outstanding Communist Youth League members and outstanding Communist Youth League cadres. Jin Dingdu, a member of the presidium, led members of the student union to learn the May Fourth spirit. By studying the May Fourth spirit, members of the student union have a deep understanding of the core content of the May Fourth spirit of patriotism, progress, democracy, and science. They realize that the May Fourth spirit can help young people "encourage their feelings for their family and country and forge ahead with the motherland; adhere to the unity of knowledge and action, and unite with the people." With the requirements and expectations of "working together", in the future life, with the self of youth and the self of struggle, we will pave the way and build bridges for national rejuvenation and contribute to the construction of the motherland.

## Reduce the burden of "heart" and walk in pursuit of light - 2302 Psychology Class Meeting

Senior Jin Dingdu came to the podium with his rich experience. **His words are like the spring breeze blowing on your face, warm and full of power**. He shared his experiences and insights when facing pressure, and told us how to adjust our mentality and how to find solutions to problems. His sharing made us deeply understand that pressure is not a scourge, but the trials and tests on the road to growth. **As long as we face it bravely and respond positively, we can turn pressure into motivation to move forward**.

## Academy of Biological Sciences 2102 Youth League Branch Warm Winter Story Exchange Meeting

The second person to share was classmate Jin Dingdu. He started his sharing from three parts: "Unforgettable love between teachers", "Renewal of the tide" and "Looking at the beauty of my hometown". Jin Dingdu returned to his alma mater to visit his mentor. At the same time, he shared his preparation plan and study methods with his juniors and students, and provided his insights on life attitudes and future choices; Jin Dingdu also visited the 40th anniversary exhibition of Guangdong’s reform and opening up. , in-depth study and understanding of the party’s theory and achievements; finally, he also introduced the Spring Festival customs of Ruian, his hometown, Wenzhou, Zhejiang.
